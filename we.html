Empowering Communities

We consult pro-bono for nonprofits around Atlanta, accelerating their success by developing innovating solutions to a variety of problems. Our students are passionate about giving back to the community and dedicated to changing people's lives.

Developing Leaders

Everyone in SCNO is place on a project in a role that best matches their skill level. The structure of the organization allows for members to quickly gain leadership skills.

Delivering Results

The project culminates with the team working on a final presentation and a detailed final report for the client to ensure that the project was meaningul and the most impactful.
